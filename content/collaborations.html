InfoXpand Consortium

We are exploring and modeling the interaction of infectious disease and social dynamics in the context of the infodemic. In particular, we investigate the interactions with information, awareness, opinions, behaviour and mobility. This consortium is funded by the Federal Ministry for Education and Research, Germany.
