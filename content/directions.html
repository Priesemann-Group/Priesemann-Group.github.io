How to find my office.

Office number 3.124

My office is in the upmost corner of the MPI, facing north.
To get there:
– enter the main building, and go to the upmost floor (2nd floor)
– find the bridge that connects the main building with the annex, close to room 2.11
– in the annex, go to the upmost (3rd) floor
– my office is at the end of one of the four corridors, facing away from the main building

 

Getting to the MPI for Dynamics and Self-Organization (MPI-DS)

Address: Am Fassberg 17, 37077 Gottingen

By bus – from the train station or city center, take any of the bus lines 21/22/23 in direction of Fassberg or Nikolausberg. Exit at the stop Fassberg and turn left into the street “Am Fassberg” to reach the Max Planck Campus (i.e. the only group of buildings you see in the vicinity of the bus station). The MPI-DS is a new building on your right hand side, uphill. The bus ride takes about 20 minutes.

By taxi – take one of the taxis that is waiting in front of the train station. The trip will take about 15 minutes and cost about 15-20 Euros.
